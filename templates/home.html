SkinScreen

The first step to taking charge of your skin health

How it Works



Upload an Image of Your Skin Concern

You can upload an image of your skin concern. Please check out our “FAQ” page for information on how to take the best image for the most accurate prediction. If you receive a low SPF, you may need to retake the image.



Receive a Classification for Your Skin Concern

We will run the image through our SkinScreen model to classify your skin concern and provide a SkinScreen Prediction Factor (SPF), which tells you how confident we are in the classification. We do not store this image in any databases.



View Hospitals in Your Area

If you input a zip code along with your image, we will give you a list of recommended hospitals in your area. These hospitals are not sponsored. We use publicly available data from US News Health on the best hospitals.



Why Start With Us?

There is so much skin health information online, and many skin conditions have similar appearances and symptoms, so it can be difficult to narrow down what you need to visit a doctor for and go into the appointment feeling informed. We can help with that. With your SkinScreen classification, you can do more specific research on the identified skin concern, find doctors that specialize in it, and be more informed walking into your appointment.
