Is SkinScreen an alternative to going to the doctor?

No! You can think of SkinScreen as a similar concept to sunscreen. Sunscreen is a preventative first step to help you take care of your skin health, but if you have a concern, you should also visit a doctor. SkinScreen works in the same way - weâ€™re here to help you take care of your skin health by helping you become better informed about your skin concern. But, you should also always visit a doctor if you have a skin concern.

How do I take a good picture to upload to the website?

How do I take a good picture to upload to the website?

  1. Make sure you have a good quality camera (phone cameras work great for this!)
  2. Make sure to take a picture in good lighting: flash or bright natural lighting are preferred. If you have a macro mode to take pictures on your phone, that would be even more helpful for classification.
  3. Make sure your entire skin concern is in the frame and in focus.
  4. Try to limit the image to just one skin concern. If there are multiple concerns in the same image, it will be difficult to classify.
